STELLA MIN, Ph.D.

Denver, CO · stella.n.min@outlook.com

I earned my B.A. in Sociology and Economics from the University of Colorado Denver and Ph.D. in Sociology (emphasis in demography) from Florida State University. As a graduate researcher, I was awarded multiple prestigious grants and scholarships, including the National Science Foundation Research Fellowship, Population Research Bureau Policy Communication Fellowship, and Nationational Institutes of Health Predoctoral Fellowship.

Prior to my post-secondary education, I started a marketing and advertising company that served small Korean businesses around the country. I provided business insights, built websites, developed and managed digital and print strategies, and facilitated relationships between business leaders and investors.

Currently, I am a Data Scientist for IBM Watson Health, Life Sciences Division, where I design and implement predictive models to identify trends and actionable insights for companies around the world. Key responsibilities include conceptualizing, evaluating, and communicating technical procedures and results to technical and non-technical audiences. In a typical day, I implement complex analyses using big data frameworks to identify root causes of trends and actionable insights, including competitive intelligence, while actively engaging stakeholders throughout all phases of projects. I also routinely collaborate with cross-functional Product Development Teams to design and evaluate scalable, data solutions and strategies for B2B services and products.

Generally, I am steeped in research and data, whether I am at work or at home because of my insatiable desire to learn more about the world around me. If I am not reading about or tinkering with the latest analytical tech, you will probably catch me reading a book, listening to a podcast, or watching an educational video--and maybe some late night comedy in between. Beyond learning new things, I enjoy exploring remote destinations and playboard games with friends and family. I also enjoy crafty hobbies such as knitting, crocheting, embroidery, sketching, and paper crafts.
